Current Research

I am working with my advisor Dr. David Pan along with Dr. Allen Gary and Dr. Qiang Hu from the Centre for Space Plasma and Aeronomic Research (CSPAR) at UAH to apply Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to Solar Physics and Space Weather related problems. Our current research focuses on using Deep CNNs to extract features from images of the Sun collected by the NASA SDO mission in order to come up with better magnetic field models.
